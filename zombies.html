ZOMBIES

Zombie Codename Image Notes
Basic Zombie Regular old zombie. Avoid bites and scratches. Motivations seem to be to consume brains. Low intelligence. Slow. Some have been spotted wearing floaties, allowing them to float on water. Leading theory as to where they were obtained is Lake Ontario.
Flag Zombie Similar to the regular zombie. Uses a flag to signify that a large horde of zombies is approaching. Moves slightly faster than normal zombies.
Conehat Zombie Regular zombie that has decided to put a traffic cone on. Traffic cone absorbs a good amount of damage. Be wary.
Buckethat Zombie Regular zombie that has decided to put a bucket on its head. The bucket seems to have a streak of blood on it. The bucket absorbs a large amount of damage. Magnets seem to be effective in taking the bucket off.
Pole Vaulting Zombie Capable of jumping over most plants. Runs pretty fast before jumping. Tall-nut blocks this. Was likely a pole vaulter before becoming zombified. Evidently wasn't that good, since Tall-Nuts aren't that tall.
Newspaper Zombie Seems to have been reading the newspaper before being zombified. Appears to rely on newspaper to function. Newspaper blocks shots from Peashooters. When newspaper is broken, zombie becomes enraged and speeds up.
Screen Door Zombie Regular zombie that picked up a screen door to act as a shield. Screen door is thwarted by catapult plants. Magnets have also been effective in taking it away. Still absorbs a large amount of damage.
Football Zombie Was likely a football player before being zombified. Equipment absorbs a huge amount of damage. Highly recommended to either use a Potato Mine to blow it up or a magnet to get rid of its armour. Quite speedy as well. Leading theory is that they were bitten on the forearm, since that is the only bit of exposed skin.
Zomboni Zamboni probably stolen from Scotiabank Arena. Zamboni seems to immediately crumble when rolling over spikes of any kind. Other than that, it is extremely tough, and leaves an ice trail behind wherever it goes.
Balloon Zombie Zombie that is floating due to a balloon. Wind Clover can blow it away. If you don't have Wind Clover, use a sharp throwable object, such as a spear or stick, to try and pop the balloon.
Ladder Zombie Carpenter zombie that carries aroudn a ladder to climb over specifically defensive plants. Magnets can take this ladder away. Otherwise, it also absorbs a good amount of damage, but can be evaded through catapult plants.
Catapult Zombie Zombie that hides near the back of the horde and throws catapulted basketballs. Can be mitigated by having a roof. Note that this may impede catapult plants.
Gargantuan Zombie Gigantic zombie with a massive amount of health. Once half its health is done, it will throw a small midget zombie very far. Will instantly crush all plants within reach. If you see this, either focus everything onto it or run. Explosives are effective against this monster.
Midget Zombie Tiny zombie thrown by Gargantuan Zombie when it is at half health. It is small and nimble, but does not have that much health. Don't stop paying attention to it though.
